# Component licenses

Evo is a workspace composed of separately licensed components. There is no
single root license that overrides their terms.

- Chromium and the Chromium-derived Evo browser shell retain Chromium's license
  and third-party notices in the Chromium checkout.
- Evo Runtime is maintained in the private `evo-runtime` repository.
- Evo OpenCode retains OpenCode's upstream license and notices in the
  `evo-opencode` repository.
- The deprecated SwiftUI/WKWebView application retains its GPL-3.0 license and
  third-party notices on branch `legacy/webkit` and tag `webkit-final`.
